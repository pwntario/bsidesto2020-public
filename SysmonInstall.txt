1) Download Sysmon64.exe and BSides-Sysmon.xml from https://github.com/pwntario/bsidesto2020-public
2) Save the files in the same directory
3) Open an administrative command prompt
4) Navigate to the directory where your Sysmon64.exe and BSides-Sysmon.xml are saved
5) Run: Sysmon64.exe -i BSides-Sysmon.xml -accepteula
6) To Update the Sysmon Config: Sysmon64.exe -c BSides-Sysmon.xml
